FAQ

Common Questions

Can you always recover my data?
Not always — recovery success depends on the type and extent of damage. We'll give you an honest assessment after diagnosis. We never charge for recovery we can't complete.
My phone screen is broken but it still turns on — can you get my photos?
Yes, in many cases. If the device still powers on, data recovery is often straightforward. Bring it in and we'll take a look.
My phone got water damaged — is my data gone?
Not necessarily. Water damage recovery is possible in many cases, especially if you act quickly and don't try to turn it on repeatedly. Bring it in as soon as possible.
How long does data recovery take?
Simple transfers and recoveries can be done same-day. More complex cases involving damaged hardware may take longer.
What if you can't recover my data?
We'll let you know upfront after the diagnostic. If recovery isn't possible, you only pay the $60 diagnostic fee — no charge for unsuccessful recovery attempts.
