Signs You Need a New Battery

Is Your Battery Failing?

🔋

Drains Too Fast

Battery dropping 20–30% per hour with light use is a sign of battery degradation.

📴

Random Shutoffs

Phone turning off at 20–30% battery is a classic sign the battery has failed.

🔥

Gets Hot Quickly

Excessive heat during charging or normal use often points to a degraded battery.

⏱️

Won't Hold a Charge

Needing to charge multiple times a day? Time for a new battery.

📱

Swollen Battery

A bulging back cover or screen lifting is a sign of a swollen battery — get it replaced immediately.

🐢

Slow Performance

iPhones with degraded batteries throttle performance. A new battery restores full speed.

How long does battery replacement take?
Most battery replacements are done in 45 minutes to 1.5 hours depending on the device. We'll give you an estimate when you come in.
How do I know if my battery needs replacement?
On iPhone, go to Settings → Battery → Battery Health. If it's below 80%, replacement is recommended. Android users can bring the device in and we'll check it for you.
Will I lose my data?
No. Battery replacement doesn't affect your apps or data in any way.
