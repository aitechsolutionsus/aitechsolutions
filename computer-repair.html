FAQ

Common Questions

How long does computer repair take?
Simple repairs like virus removal or OS reinstall are often done same-day or next-day. Hardware repairs depend on parts availability.
Will you lose my data?
We take every precaution to protect your data. For repairs requiring a reinstall, we back up your data first whenever possible.
Is it worth repairing an older computer?
Often yes! A virus removal or SSD upgrade can make a 5-year-old computer feel brand new at a fraction of the replacement cost. We'll give you an honest assessment.
Do you fix Macs?
Yes! We service all Mac models including the newer M1, M2, and M3 chip Macs.
Do I need an appointment?
No appointment needed. Walk in Mon–Fri 10AM–6PM. Calling ahead helps us confirm parts if needed.

Computer Running Slow or Not Working?

Bring it in Mon–Fri 10AM–6PM or give us a call. We'll diagnose the issue and give you an honest recommendation.
